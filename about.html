🚀 Alien Invasion Defense 🛸

Welcome to Alien Invasion Defense 👾

Alien Invasion Defense is an exciting space-shooter game that challenges you to defend your base from waves of alien invaders. With sleek graphics, easy controls, and thrilling gameplay, you’ll be immersed in an intergalactic battle for survival. Are you ready to become the ultimate space defender?

🌟 Features

  • ✨ Exciting Alien Invasion Gameplay: Fight off waves of alien ships that threaten your base.
  • 🎮 Easy Controls & Interface: Simple controls make gameplay intuitive and accessible for all players.
  • 💥 Three Lives: Protect your base with only 3 chances before the game ends.
  • 🚀 Smooth Shooting: Responsive shooting mechanics to destroy incoming alien invaders.
  • 🖥️ Modern Graphics: A visually pleasing, clean interface that complements the game’s exciting pace.
  • 🔄 Quick Restart: Restart the game instantly with one click.
  • 🏆 Leaderboard (Optional): Track your highest scores and challenge your friends to beat them.

🎮 How to Play

  • Start the Game: Click the "Start" button to begin your defense mission.
  • Move and Shoot: Use the Arrow Keys or on-screen controls to move your spaceship and shoot alien ships.
  • Lives: You have 3 lives. You lose a life if an alien reaches your base or if your spaceship is hit.
  • Game Over: The game ends when you lose all 3 lives. Click "Restart" to try again!
  • Progress: Destroy all alien ships in each wave to advance to the next level and increase your score.

📜 Game Rules

  • Survive as Long as Possible: Protect your base by shooting alien ships and avoiding their attacks.
  • Three Lives Only: If you lose all your lives (by aliens reaching your base or hitting your ship), the game ends.
  • Restart Anytime: Use the "Restart" button to start over and try to achieve a higher score.
  • Earn Points: Destroy alien ships to accumulate points and aim for the highest score possible.

Tech Stack ⚙️

Alien Invasion Defense is powered by modern web technologies to ensure a smooth and engaging experience for players. Here’s the tech stack behind the game:

  • HTML5 - Structure and game layout.
  • CSS3 - For styling and responsive design.
  • JavaScript - For dynamic game logic and interactivity.
  • Canvas API - For rendering smooth game animations.
