Alien Invasion Defense - FAQ

1. What is Alien Invasion Defense ? ▼

Alien Invasion is an action-packed web game where players defend Earth from waves of alien invaders. Using advanced weaponry, players must eliminate enemies and survive increasingly difficult levels.

2. How do I play Alien Invasion? ▼

  • Move: Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character.
  • Shoot: Use the spacebar or left mouse button to fire at the aliens.
  • Power-Ups: Collect power-ups to enhance your abilities and stay in the game longer.

3. What are the system requirements? ▼

  • Browsers: Latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari.
  • Devices: Desktops, laptops, and most tablets with a modern web browser.

4. How do I score points? ▼

Points are awarded based on the number of aliens you destroy. Defeating larger or more powerful aliens gives you higher points. Stay alive and defeat more aliens to climb the leaderboard!

5. Can I save my progress? ▼

Currently, Alien Invasion is a single-session game. Once you exit the game or refresh the page, your progress will be lost. Stay tuned for future updates!

6. Where can I find more information about the game? ▼

Refer to the game instructions for detailed gameplay rules and tips
